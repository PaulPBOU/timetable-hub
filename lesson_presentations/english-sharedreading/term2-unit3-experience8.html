The Great Escape:
Witness Interviews

Role Play and Famous Scenes

Breaking News

We have just received a phone call at the zoo. A member of the public claims they have spotted the escaped animals!

Your job: We need our best zookeepers to interview the witness. You must gather as much information as possible to write an official incident report.

Role Play Time

Pair up. One person is the Zookeeper, the other is the Witness. Zookeepers, make sure you take notes on your whiteboards!

03:00

When the timer finishes, swap roles so everyone has a turn taking notes.

The Official Report

Now, work independently. Using the notes you took during the interview, write up an official incident report from the perspective of the zookeeper.

  • Include the witness's details.
  • Describe the animal and its actions accurately.
  • Write in complete sentences using professional language.
